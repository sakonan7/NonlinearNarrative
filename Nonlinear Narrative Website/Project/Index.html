For the first assignment of ARS 327, I made a nonlinear narrative out of my path through college. From my experiences writing one of my stories, The Tower, in 2017, I developed the idea of creating a nonlinear narrative with some linearity. This web site tells a nonlinear story of my journey as an aspiring game designer and story writer. Click on any of the images to start to view a timeline of events leading to 2018.
